Complete the <a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Waterfall_Quest/Quick_guide' target='_blank'>Waterfall Quest</a> to get an immediate 13,750 experience boost to both Attack and Strength. At level 1, this is an immediate boost to <a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Experience#Levels_1-99' target='_blank'>level 30</a>.
<a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Count_Check' target='_blank'>Count Check</a> offers a one-time instant teleport from Lumbridge to the Stronghold of Security, where you will earn 10,000 GP upon completion. Spam click the doors to skip the questions.
Talk to the <a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Ranged_combat_tutor' target='_blank'>Ranged combat tutor</a> in Lumbridge to set arrows to automatically go into your quiver when they are picked up off the ground.
Attack increases hit chance. Strength increases the highest number you can hit. Defense decreases an opponent's chance to hit. Neither Defense nor defensive bonuses decrease what an opponent can hit.
Purchase an Amulet of Glory and Warrior's Bracelet as soon as possible. They are BiS (best-in-slot) for a very long time, and have no level requirements.
Looting combat zones in PvP worlds is an excellent and easy source of money if you're building up from nothing. Players that die or kill another player often panic-grab the highest value items and leave the rest to eventually become visible to the public.
To afk-train Magic quickly, meet a friend in the <a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Clan_Wars#Free_for_all' target='_blank'>Clan Wars</a> free-for-all area and repeatedly auto-cast <a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Fire_Strike' target='_blank'>Fire Strike</a> on each other. Drink <a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Jug_of_wine' target='_blank'>wine</a> to heal.
<a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Ardougne' target='_blank'>Ardougne</a> is an excellent area to train Thieving. To get there, bank all of your items and gear in Edgeville and pull the <a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Ardougne_teleport_lever' target='_blank'>lever</a> in the ruins south of the bank. It will lead you to the wilderness, and then back to Ardougne when pulled again.
Read the <a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/New_player_guide' target='_blank'>New Player Guide</a> on the official OSRS Wiki as soon as possible. Although it assumes the player is F2P and not P2P, it still has a lot of vital information.
Don't bury your bones! Instead, use a public house in the <a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/House_party' target='_blank'>House Party</a> world. Using your bones on their altar yields a free +350% Prayer experience multiplier, and it's automatic! Un-note bones using <a href='https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Phials' target='_blank'>Phials</a> nearby.
